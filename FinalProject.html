Macarons

Macarons are the perfect gift for a happy occasion or just as a special treat.

Colorful, pillowy, and delicately flavored, macarons are perhaps one of the most famous and treasured French desserts. This meringue-based sweet comes in many tastes and colors, from classic salted caramel to lavender and honey to the wildly fanciful fig and foie gras.
The word macaron comes from the Italian word Maccherone which means fine dough. These delicate cookie like confections are made with almond flour, confectioner's sugar and egg whites. Our special technique makes them delicately crunchy on the outside, and soft and chewy on the inside.
